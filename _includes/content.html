Who am I?

My name is Krzysztof. I'm an iOS developer and a cultural anthropologist living in Warsaw, Poland. I work at Polidea.
As an enthusiast of developer communities, I coorganize Mobile Warsaw meetup. As a developer, I strive for the clients happiness and code cleanliness. As an cultural anthropologist, I strongly believe the focus must be put on the users and their needs. I value qualitative research over arbitrary vision.
I have some thoughts on culture and programming if you're interested. Please let me know what you think @culturalcoder.
